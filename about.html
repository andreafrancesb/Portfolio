aboutme

Andrea Brown is the founder of Reliabl, an inclusive moderation solution for community platforms.

They are also a full-stack developer in training, and published researcher on the topics of AI, Blockchain, and equity in tech, as well as an Entrepreneur in Residence at UC San Diego.

Andrea's work on equitable content moderation has been featured in Mashable, Huffington Post, Motherboard, and The Center for Democracy and Technology.

They have been invited to speak at several conferences including:

  • SXSW
  • International Machine Learning Conference
  • Women's Venture Summit
  • YTH Tech Summit
